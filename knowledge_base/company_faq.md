# TechNova Company FAQ

## 1. What does TechNova do?
TechNova is a fictional SaaS company that helps operations teams monitor recurring work, customer issues, and internal service requests from one workspace. Its platform combines ticket tracking, workflow automation, dashboards, and AI-assisted summaries so teams can resolve tasks faster and reduce manual follow-up. TechNova is commonly used by software companies, e-commerce brands, and IT support teams that need visibility across multiple departments.

## 2. What pricing plans does TechNova offer?
TechNova offers three standard plans: Starter, Growth, and Enterprise. The Starter plan is designed for small teams and includes up to 10 users, shared dashboards, and basic automations. Growth adds unlimited workflows, SLA tracking, and advanced analytics. Enterprise includes single sign-on, audit exports, sandbox environments, and a dedicated customer success manager. Monthly plans can be canceled any time, while annual plans are billed upfront at a discount.

## 3. Does TechNova offer a free trial?
Yes. New customers can start a 14-day free trial without entering a credit card. During the trial, users can connect email, Slack, and CSV imports, build automations, and test reporting features. At the end of the trial, the workspace becomes read-only until a paid subscription is selected. Trial data is kept for 30 days in case the customer decides to upgrade later.

## 4. How does TechNova handle security and data hosting?
TechNova encrypts data in transit and at rest, supports role-based access controls, and provides detailed audit logs on higher-tier plans. Customer data is hosted in regional cloud environments in the United States and the European Union. Enterprise customers can request a data processing addendum and configurable retention rules for archived tickets, comments, and uploaded files.

## 5. What support options are available?
Starter customers receive email support during business hours. Growth customers receive priority email support with a target first response time of four business hours. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 urgent incident support, onboarding assistance, and quarterly workflow reviews. TechNova also maintains a searchable help center with setup guides, release notes, and troubleshooting articles.
